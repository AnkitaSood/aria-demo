What format should my abstract be in?

Submit a 150–300 word abstract in plain text. Include a one-sentence summary, 3–4 bullet points covering key takeaways, and your target audience level (beginner / intermediate / advanced).

 How long is the demo slot?

Demo slots are 25 minutes including Q&A. Plan for 18–20 minutes of content with 5–7 minutes for questions. Live coding demos should have a fallback recording in case of network issues.
